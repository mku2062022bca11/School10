At QB365, we believe in holistic development. Our diverse range of co-curricular activities helps students discover their passions, develop new skills, and build confidence beyond academics.

Our comprehensive activity program includes cultural events, sports competitions, art and craft workshops, science exhibitions, and various clubs and societies. These activities are designed to complement academic learning and foster well-rounded development.

We organize regular events throughout the year including our annual day celebration, sports carnival, science fair, and cultural festivals. These events provide students with opportunities to showcase their talents, build confidence, and develop important life skills.

Our experienced activity coordinators ensure that every student has the opportunity to participate in activities that interest them and help them discover new talents and abilities.
